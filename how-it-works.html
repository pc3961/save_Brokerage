How it Works

01

Get on Board

Once you sign up with us our friendly, knowledgeable consultants do market research for you so you know what comparable homes are currently selling for in your area. We do a home evaluation to help you estimate the best price for which you can sell your home.

02

Prepare to Sell

You will get expert recommendations on any home renovations that may be necessary and we will suggest key improvements and practical tips to make your home sale-ready. Our consultants can arrange home staging for you to showcase your home in the best possible way. We ensure that all unique attributes of your home will be highlighted.

03

Meet Buyers

What sets Save on Brokerage apart is the unique and professional real estate event organized by us to help you find prospective buyers for your home. The two-day real estate event brings buyers and sellers together in a large interactive trading market where you can deal directly with each other.

04

Close The Deal

This event allows you to schedule appointments directly with potential buyers. If needed, we also guide you to conduct your own open house. Our consultants can negotiate for you to help make the sale. We can even connect you to a real estate lawyer to help close the deal smoothly. Sell your property directly to potential buyers and maximize your net profit with no broker commission.
